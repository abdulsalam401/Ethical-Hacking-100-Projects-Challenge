💡 THE CONCEPT IN 30 SECONDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Normal Shell:
  You → connect to → Victim machine
  (You initiate the connection)

Reverse Shell (This Project):
  Victim → connects back to → You
  (Victim initiates the connection) ◄── That's the "reverse"!

Why?
  • Victim connects OUT (often allowed by firewalls)
  • You get a shell prompt on the victim
  • You can execute commands remotely
  • Everything encrypted (AES-256)

This Project Teaches:
  • How it's built
  • How encryption works
  • How to detect it
  • Why it's secure (and its limitations)
